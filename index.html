How to Play
  • Speak Good: Draw a card and get your team to guess it. Each card has a short Top Word and the Full Phrase it's part of.
  • Keep It Short: Only single-syllable clue words allowed. No exceptions!
  • Watch the Club: Slip up and say a big word? Tap CLUBBED! — you lose a point and the rival team gets to yell "BAD!" (bonus points for an actual inflatable club whack).
  • Score: +1 if your team guesses the Top Word, +3 if they guess the Full Phrase, −1 if you get clubbed. Most points when time's up wins!
